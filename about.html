An important disclaimer

This site is a tongue-in-cheek demonstration of a simple black & white design idea. As this is meant to be a portfolio, the whole site has been designed to have an excessive 'Hey, look at me!' -feel to it.


I aim to be a lot less smug in real life.


Also, I have to credit Ere Männistö for his informative tutorials on building a website. Some of the tools used in creating this site were inspired by his instructional videos.


I did manage to solve a few of the encountered problems by myself as well. E.g. how to use 'height: auto;' and 'min-height: 100vh;' to prevent the content from creating white background once the content height exceeded the value of 100vh when using only 'height: 100vh;'. The video containers also use scaling based on the viewport's width.


I was also able to construct the responsive 'hamburger' -menu with examples from W3Schools and the material provided by the lecturer Henri Koukka, though I'm not fully satisfied with the result.
